Welcome to The Framing Studio and Gallery, Queenstown

Situated beneath the Coronet Peak ski field, on the outskirts of Queenstown, The Framing Studio and Gallery offers a select showing of highly acclaimed artworks. Our gallery is like no other in New Zealand. We are one of only two galleries worldwide to showcase the magnificent works of metalpoint artist - Gordon Hanley.

With constantly changing inventory, we are always seeking new artists. We have multiple works from Gordon Hanely who is recognised for his centuries-old technique known as metalpoint. His drawings are created from pure 24ct, gold metal. We have a selection of Ivan Clarke's Giclee pieces which follow the famous story of Lonely Dog. We also have a selection of print work from Joel Rea and Spike Wademan.
