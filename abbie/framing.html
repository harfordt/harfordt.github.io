A warm welcome to The Framing Studio

Early in my career I was employed by a company in Kent, England, specialising in the production of hand made, maple veneered picture frame mouldings. It was here that I told myself I would one day own a picture framing business.

Nearly 20 years later I started up The Framing Studio on the outskirts of Queenstown, New Zealand. We quickly out grew that location and in 1999 moved into the centre of town to a historic site which used to be the residence of one of Queenstown's early doctors (and former mayor), Dr Anderson. 16 years forward and once again we have outgrown that location. In january 2015 we moved to our current location in Arthur's Point. Hopefully this is big enough for our ongrowing business!

It has always been a goal to be the very best. In 2000 I achieved my Guild Commended Framer qualification. This was followed by in 2000, managed by the Fine Art Trade Guild in England. the Certified Picture Framer qualification, managed by the Professional Picture Framers Association in America in 2007. Finally in 2008, I became the 45th Master Certified Picture Framer (MCPF) worldwide. To this day I remain the only MCPF in New Zealand.

Having won more international awards than any other framer in New Zealand I am proud of my design skills and the resulting completed works I have acheived over the years. ‘They’ say “A good picture deserves a good frame whereas a bad picture needs a good frame”
