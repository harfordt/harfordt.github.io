Available Artworks

Gordon Hanley

Gordon Hanley has achieved the ability to produce works in precious metals. This 600 year old Renaissance art form, known as metalpoint, is the art of drawing with a metal stylus and, thanks to his scientific background, Gordon has progressed this delicate medium, usually in pure 24ct gold, to levels never previously achieved.

Joel Rea

Joel Rea, born 1983, graduated Queensland College of art in 2003 with a bachelor of fine arts. Rea has been acclaimed for his oil paintings throughout many art awards held across Australia. Since graduating from college, Rea has received 18 art awards throughout Australia. His paintings speak for themselves, addressing issues of our present time.

Ivan Clarke

Ivan is initially known for his powerful landscapes painted with oil on canvas. However in recent years he has become well renowned for his Lonely Dog creation.

Lonely Dog was discovered while "doodling with a paint brush". The inspiration behind the concept flowed while he wondered what his pet pooch got up to when the family departed him for vacations. Lonely Dog Art has now grown into an art collection of over 200 artworks.

Spike Wademan

Spike has turned his aim to creating realistic oil paintings after a long career in the advertising and publishing indstry.

While he is best known for his marine and aviation paintings, he has recently started to paint New Zealand's majestic mountains, inspired from 17 years of calling this place home. He has won many local art awards and also regularly teaches drawing and paintings. It has been 1oo years since the TSS Earnslaw was first lauched. This painting is of his maiden voyage.
