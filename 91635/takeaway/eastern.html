Eastern

Their eastern food is not only delicious but also in great proposition, they have dishes from everywhere like in China, Malaysia, Singapore and Thailand. When they first started they changed their recipe for the dishes a few times it got really bad at times but now the food are great. The previous owner was friendly with all the workers but the previous shop sold their meals in small proposition which quite a few people complained about. So the new owners that took over changed it to a larger proposition. They have two types of numbers one is for chinese takeaway and the other one is for fish ‘n’ chips so it may seem that you are waiting a long time but those people may be in a different line from you. We also have phone orders so when they come in pay and they may leave before you
