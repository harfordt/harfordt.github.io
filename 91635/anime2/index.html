Awesomeness02's two users go by the pen names of Inoki Akatsuki and Mikoto Yamazaki. They're a pair of twins who focus on 'Anime-style' drawings, mainly digital at that. Their username - A02 - first started out when they were choosing a username for when they wrote fanfiction, "...and named ourselves "Awesomeness". It was already taken. So, since we both decided we'd be sharing an account, and we're twins, and there's two of us, we were going to be called Awesomeness2, but that didn't look as awesome as Awesomeness02. Thus, we now have Awesomness02."
This site will mainly be focusing on Mikoto of A02, and all commentary will be by Mikoto.
