BREAKFAST

These are a few of my favourite breakfast recipes. Beneficial, nutrious and delicious! A great start to the day.

BANANA SMOOTHIE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Banana
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yoghurt
BUY HERE

METHOD

Get a blender. Put all ingrediants in blender. Blend until you have desired consistency. Add more milk if you wish to have the smoothie less thick. Enjoy.

BERRY PORRIDGE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 part Rolled Oats
  • 2 parts Water
  • Berries
  • Additional desired toppings
BUY HERE

METHOD

Put oats and water in a bowl, and microwave until you have desired consistency. Top with berries and any other things you wish to eat with your porridge. Enjoy.

AVOCADO TOAST

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 Avocado
  • Toast
  • Salt and Peper to taste
  • Additional toppings
BUY HERE

METHOD

Cook toast to how you like it. Mash the avocado on top of the toast. Add salt and peper and any other toppings you wish to add. Enjoy.
