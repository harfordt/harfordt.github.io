RASPBERRY AND APPLE CRUMBLE

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 Apples of your choice
  • 1 cup Raspberries
  • 1 cup Rolled Oats
  • 2 tbsp Sugar
  • 40g diced Butter
BUY HERE

METHOD

Preheat oven to 180 degrees. In a bowl, combine oats and sugar, and rub in diced butter. Add nuts (I added almonds and walnuts) if you desire. Peel, core and chop apples. In a baking dish place your apples and raspberries. Spread your oat mixture over the top and cook for 45 - 60 minutes. Serve with custard or ice cream if you want and enjoy!
