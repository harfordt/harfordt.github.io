Cardrona

Cardrona. Trail Map.

Cardrona is a relaxed and friendly ski area that has been putting smiles on skiers and snowboarders for 35 years. Explore it all - wide open beginner trails, pristine groomers, fun freeride terrain and world class parks and pipes. Whatever your level, we’ve got the snow for you!

Parks and Pipes. Superpipe.

Ready to take some park laps!? Cardrona has the most extensive park & pipe facilities in the Southern Hemisphere with options for all levels of park riders. Whether you’re a pro, or looking to take your first laps through the beginner park, you’re all sorted in Cardrona Parks.

Olympic Superpipe

The 22ft Olympic Superpipe is a stunt ditch with a reputation as big as its walls. Cardrona’s largest halfpipe is also the largest in New Zealand and one of the very few 22ft tall options in the Southern Hemisphere. Serviced by its very own surface tow (named after Cardrona’s own Wells brothers), the opportunity to lap this all day has pipe jocks frothing! The pure scope and size of this beast makes it a hotspot for pros to train on. This unique feature is the same size as the pipes cut for the X Games and Winter Olympics.
