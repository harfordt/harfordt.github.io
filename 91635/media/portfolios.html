Portfolios

As part of the NCEA school curriculum, each year I produce a portfolio of work. These works are varied, but they all centre around one theme. The idea of the portfolio is to be able to develop this idea using art.

2014

In 2014 I completed a painting portfolio for NCEA Level 1. The theme for this portfolio was about the balance between technology and nature.

2015

My portfolio for NCEA Level 2 was a design portfolio. The brief we were given instructed us to design and promote a film festival. My festival was called "Girls" and featured Girl, Interrupted, Carrie, and Heathers. These films helped inspire the design of my portfolio.
