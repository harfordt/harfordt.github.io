Traditional Media

Traditional media was what I first started out with. Pencils, paper, paints, charcoal, pastels, I've experimented with a variety of things. This is just some of the many things I have created over the years. Despite using digital media more often now, I still keep a visual diary and draw in it often. I also use traditional media for a lot of my work at school.

2013 - 2016

The volume of my art went down quite a bit in this time. I was much busier and had less time to myself in which to just create. However I still did art for schoolwork, as well as a small amount in my own time. A lot of the work from these more recent years is work I am really proud of.

2009 - 2012

These works span from the end of primary, through intermediate school and into my first year at college. This is around the time I was first introduced to Photoshop.

2008 and earlier

In the mid 2000's was when I first started keeping a visual diary. Most of my drawings had no purpose and were just a creative outlet. I also attended weekly art classes, but as I was just starting out, I find it funny to look back and see how awful some of my work from back then is. However I still often look through my old visual diaries, and it makes me proud to see how much I have improved.
