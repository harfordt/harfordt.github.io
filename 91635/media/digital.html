Digital Media

More recently, I have begun to create more digital art. As it is still fairly new, I often just produce small doodles to help build my skills and familiarity with the programs. However I also use it quite often at school, and so have created a fair bit of stuff already. Some are just drawing, while others are a combination of digital drawings, traditional media, and photos.

Zine

As part of a recent school project I created a zine about safe sex. This task involved using establshed design practices. I used a variety of mixed media to creat imagery, which I then further developed with the use of the Adoobe Suite. I then composed my pages in Photoshop, before printing them and hand stitching the spine.

Doodles

I often do small doodles or drawings of randowm things, as I am still getting used to drawing digitally. Often these small doodles are just to practice certain skills, or I may have been inspired by something in particular.
