Travellers Experiences

Kenya

For me, this trip to northern Kenya was a real adventure. Driving through desert, past huge rocks and extinct volcanoes, across great spreads of ancient lava, passing almost no other vehicles, and then to see, through the dust, the shimmering expanse of Lake Turkana. With its amazing bush and animal life, it was really a sight to behold. Add to this the range of local groups who survive in so harsh an environment, and you have a really memorable experience.

Anne F, NZ

Indonesia

We are now back at home, reflecting on what was truely the best vacation we have ever enjoyed. From start to finish, it was a truly amazing and eyeopening experience and every day brought with it new wonders and surprises. All accommodation was either good or adequate as planned and researched. Guides and drivers were dependable and punctual, and we had an unforgettable experience. Couldn't have done it without your help!

Frank and Nicole Smith, UK

Italy & Greece
