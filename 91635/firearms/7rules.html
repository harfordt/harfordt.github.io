7 Firearms Safety Rules

T.A.L.I.C.S.A

T.A.L.I.C.S.A is an acronym that is used to remember the 7 most basic and important rules to know when around or using a firearm

  • T: Treat every firearm as loaded
  • A: Always point the fire arm in a safe direction
  • L: Load a fire arm only when ready to fire
  • I: Identify your target beyond all doubt
  • C: Check your firing zone
  • S: Store firearms and ammunition safely and seperatly
  • A: Avoid drugs and alcohol when using firearms, they impare vision and decision making
