So I see you haven't left yet

I'm really flattered.

Journey forth and you shall see photos from all walks of life. From people to nature, day to night, power lines that seem deep and meaningful (though they really are just power lines). My philosophy is that if you take enough pictures, you're bound to get some good ones. That's why I often go trigger happy on the shutter button. It's a real problem because it's a pain-staking process deleting all the rubbish photos. Anyways go on and look at my stuff (only if you want). Oh and get in touch with me if you have anything to say (again, only if you want). You can find me at school 5 days a week.
